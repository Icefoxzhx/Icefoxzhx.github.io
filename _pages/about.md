I am Hongxin Zhang (张洪鑫), a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at [UMass Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/), advised by Prof. [Chuang Gan](https://people.csail.mit.edu/ganchuang/). Before joining UMass Amherst, I received my bachelor’s degree from [ACM Honors Class](https://acm.sjtu.edu.cn/home), [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/). I am curious about _Intelligence_ and _Life_. It's my dream to bring human-level Artificial Intelligence into reality, and have a better understanding of _human brain_. 

I'm broadly interested in studying **Artificial Intelligence**, and related disciplinary including **Cognitive Science**. My current research interests include **Embodied Intelligence**, **Multimodal Perception**, and **NLP**.
