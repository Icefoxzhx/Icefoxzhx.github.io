I am Hongxin Zhang (张洪鑫), a senior undergraduate researcher from [ACM Honors Class](https://acm.sjtu.edu.cn/home), [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/). I am curious about _Intelligence_ and *Life*. It's my dream to bring human-level Artificial Intelligence into reality, and have a better understanding of _human brain_. 

I'm broadly interested in studying **Artificial Intelligence**, and related disciplinary including **Cognitive Science**. My current research interests lie in analyzing the **robustness** and **limitations** of powerful large language models, and **multimodal learning**.

Currently, I’m fortunate to work with Prof. [Diyi Yang](https://cs.stanford.edu/~diyiy/index.html) at [Stanford](https://www.stanford.edu/) as a research intern.
